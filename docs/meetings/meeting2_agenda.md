# Meeting 2: Decision Review and Finalize Brief
Team: Squad — Section 02, Team 9
Date/Time: 2026-03-10
Goal of the meeting: Review evidence artifacts, agree on a final recommendation, and finalize the Decision Brief and Action Plan.

## 1) Status check (5 min)
- What artifacts are complete?
- What is blocked?

## 2) Evidence walkthrough (20–25 min)
- What does it show (one sentence)?
- Why does it matter to the decision?

## 3) Recommendation drafting (10–15 min)
Draft 1–3 recommendation bullets.

## 4) Action plan finalization (10 min)
Confirm owners, due dates, and definitions of done for remaining deliverables.

## 5) Wrap (2 min)
Confirm the plan, finalize the Decision Brief, and assign remaining tasks.
